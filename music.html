Music

Music is a strong thing or sound that brings us together and it also gives us a sense of belonging .Like when you are feeling down and you start listening to music ,you start dancing and all the sadness starts to disappear 

 

Reduce stress and alleviate pain
From ancient traditions to modern therapy, music has proven to be a powerful tool for emotional and physical healing. Research shows that music can trigger the release of endorphins, the body’s natural “feel-good” hormones, which help reduce stress and alleviate pain.
 

What Is Music?

 

Music is a universal language that transcends borders, cultures, and generations. It has the power to evoke emotions, tell stories, and bring people together. But what exactly is music, and why does it hold such significance in our lives?
